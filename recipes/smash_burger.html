Smash Burgers

One of the best options for a barbeque

Made from lean ground beef, these are some of the best burgers you'll eat!

These burgers are some of the best kinds to have at a cookout, barbeque, or any place where a grill is involved.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound 85% lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup grated sweet onion (from 1 small onion)
  • 2 tablespoons roasted, peeled, and chopped Hatch chiles (from fresh or thawed frozen chiles)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup canola oil, divided
  • 8 white American cheese slices
  • 4 brioche hamburger buns, split
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 6 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 6 tablespoons Hatch Chile Salsa

Directions:

  1. Using your hands, combine the ground beef with onion, chiles, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl or container until evenly incorporated. Shape into 8 equal sized balls.

  2. Heat a large cast-iron skillet or griddle over high heat until its smoking. Add 2 tablespoons oil. Add 4 meatballs and immediately flatten them to around 1/4 inch thickness with a wide and sturdy spatula. Cook until the bottoms are crisp and a deep brown, about 1 minute. Flip the patties, top each with 1 slice of cheese, and cook until bottoms are well charred and the cheese is melted, 45 seecondns to 1 minute. Remove from skillet, and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining 2 tablespoons oil, remaining 4 meatballs, and remaining 4 cheese slices.

  3. Brush the cut sides of buns with butter. With the pan, skillet, or griddle mentioned previously, toast the cut sides of the buns. Toast until buns are a bit crisp. Spread each bottom bun with 1 1/2 tablespoons mayonnaise, top with 2 patties, and spoon 1 1/2 tablespoons Hatch chile salsa over top. Cover with top bun halves and serve immediately.
